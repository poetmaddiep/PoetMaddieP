What I Offer

Workshops

I provide hands-on poetry workshops for schools, community groups, and organizers. Each session is tailored to the audience and focuses on self-expression, healing, and creativity.

Poetry Performances

As a featured poet, I deliver impactful and moving performances that resonate with diverse audiences. Book me for your next event to inspire and engage your attendees.

Guest Speaking

With a focus on mental health advocacy in Black and Brown communities, I offer talks on healing, empowerment, and change. From schools to conferences, I aim to leave an impact.

Pricing is available upon request. Priority is given to Black and Brown community organizers, schools, and nonprofits, with flexible pricing options within reason.
