About Maddie P.

Poet, Advocate, Dreamer

My Journey

Hi, I’m Maddie, a spoken word artist whose poetry is deeply rooted in themes of mental health, trauma, and resilience. My journey started when I found solace in words during my darkest moments, and poetry became my lifeline. Over the years, I’ve performed at various events, collaborated with inspiring organizations, and discovered the power of vulnerability through art.

What Drives Me

Living with mental health struggles hasn’t been easy, but it’s given me a unique perspective. My poetry is my voice, my therapy, and my rebellion against silence. I aim to shed light on topics that often remain hidden in the shadows—whether it’s the weight of depression or the healing power of love. If my words can help even one person feel seen, my mission is fulfilled.

Accomplishments

Over the years, I’ve been fortunate to perform for organizations such as XYZ Mental Health Foundation and the National Poetry Alliance. I’ve also had the privilege of performing at venues like The Grand Slam Poetry Festival and collaborating with fellow artists to raise awareness about mental health.

  • Featured Poet: The Grand Slam Poetry Festival
  • Collaboration with XYZ Mental Health Foundation
  • Tedx Talk: "Poetry, Healing, and Breaking Stigmas"

Looking Forward

I’m constantly dreaming of new ways to reach people through my poetry and advocacy. In the future, I hope to expand my workshops, collaborate with more schools and community organizations, and use my voice to continue championing mental health awareness. Every performance, every poem, and every conversation is a step towards creating a world where vulnerability is celebrated.
