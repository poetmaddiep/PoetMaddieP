About Me

I’m Maddie P, a Houston-based performance poet and creative educator. I write with the intent to speak difficult truths and hold space for transformation. My work often explores mental health, identity, grief, and the quiet joys of survival.

As the former Houston Youth Poet Laureate, I’ve performed for audiences big and small—on TEDx stages, in classrooms, and at local community events. My poem "To Be a Black Girl" was published in The New York Times and continues to resonate with readers and listeners alike.
