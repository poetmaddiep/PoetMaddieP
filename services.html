What I Offer

I provide interactive poetry workshops, live performances, guest readings, and guest judging for slams and showcases. Whether you're a school, nonprofit, or artistic collective, my goal is to uplift voices and build creative confidence through language and storytelling.

Featured Performer

Book me to perform powerful, engaging spoken word poetry at your next event, whether it’s a showcase, festival, or assembly. Each performance is tailored to the audience and crafted to inspire, challenge, and move.

Guest Judging & Readings

As a former Youth Poet Laureate and TEDx speaker, I bring experience, care, and intentionality to every judging panel and featured reading. I'm available to serve as a guest judge for poetry slams or as a guest reader for your class, event, or creative space.
