Offerings

I bring poetry to life through dynamic performances and interactive workshops that center vulnerability, truth-telling, and joy. My offerings are ideal for schools, nonprofits, universities, and community events.

Workshops

I offer writing workshops tailored to different age groups and experience levels. Each session is grounded in community-building, craft, and empowerment—participants walk away with new tools and new confidence in their voice.

Featured Performances

As a performance poet, I deliver emotionally resonant readings that combine lyricism, activism, and presence. Whether you’re organizing a festival, rally, or private event, I bring the words—and the fire—to every mic.

Guest Judging & Readings

I’m available to serve as a guest judge for youth and adult poetry competitions, as well as a featured reader or panelist for literary events. I’m especially passionate about centering marginalized voices and fostering equity in literary spaces.
